Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Overview

ClipBot is a creator-controlled workflow for preparing and publishing short-form livestream highlight videos. This policy explains what data ClipBot may process.

Data We Process

  • OAuth tokens for connected creator accounts, including TikTok when authorized.
  • Generated video files while they are pending review, upload, or publishing.
  • Caption, title, hashtag, source credit, and publishing status metadata.
  • Basic account information returned by connected platforms when required for authorization.

How Data Is Used

Data is used only to let the authenticated creator review, upload, publish, and track their own approved clips. ClipBot does not sell personal data.

Storage And Deletion

Generated video files may be deleted automatically after successful publishing. OAuth tokens are stored only as needed to keep the creator's integration connected.

Third-Party Services

ClipBot may interact with connected publishing platforms such as TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, or Reddit after the creator authorizes access.

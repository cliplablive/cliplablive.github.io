ClipLab Live Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Overview

ClipLab Live is a creator publishing platform for preparing and publishing short-form livestream highlight videos. This policy explains what data may be processed when creators connect publishing accounts and use the platform.

Data We Process

  • OAuth tokens for connected creator accounts, including TikTok when authorized.
  • Generated video files while they are pending upload, publishing, or deletion.
  • Caption, title, hashtag, source credit, and publishing status metadata.
  • Basic account information returned by connected platforms when required for authorization.

How Data Is Used

Data is used to let authenticated creators or authorized publishing teams upload, publish, and track approved clips on connected accounts. ClipLab Live does not sell personal data.

Storage And Deletion

Generated video files may be deleted automatically after successful publishing. OAuth tokens are stored only as needed to keep authorized integrations connected. Users may request token removal or account disconnection by contacting support.

Third-Party Services

ClipLab Live may interact with connected publishing platforms such as TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram after the creator authorizes access. Each connected platform may process data under its own terms and privacy policy.

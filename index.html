What The Platform Does

ClipLab Live provides a workflow for preparing short video highlights from creator livestreams and distributing approved clips to connected social accounts. The platform focuses on vertical formatting, readable captions, metadata, and reliable upload tracking.

TikTok Integration

The TikTok integration uses OAuth authorization and the Content Posting API so an authenticated creator or authorized publishing team can upload approved videos to the connected TikTok account. Requested TikTok scopes are limited to basic account identification and video upload/publish permissions needed for this workflow. ClipLab Live does not request account passwords and does not publish without platform authorization.

Publishing Controls

  • OAuth is required before TikTok uploads are available.
  • Generated captions, titles, source credit, and hashtags are stored with each clip.
  • Publishing status is tracked so failed uploads can be reviewed and retried.
  • Local generated video files can be deleted after successful publishing.

Responsible Use

Users must publish only content they own, are authorized to use, or are otherwise permitted to distribute. ClipLab Live is designed for lawful creator publishing and respects platform policies, takedown requests, and source attribution requirements.
